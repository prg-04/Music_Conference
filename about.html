East African
Music Conference 2023

The East African Music Conference brings together experts, academics, and artists from the music industry in East Africa and beyond, every two years.

This year, we aim to expand our invitation list to include organizations and individuals who want to collaborate on projects that advance the music industry in East Africa, promote free culture and open knowledge.

Whether you are a musician, music producer, record label, music promoter, or involved in any aspect of the music industry, we invite you to join us this year to build stronger connections and a more vibrant music industry together.

Please contact us via email for any further questions about the East African Music Conference 2023

eamc2023@musicconferenceea.com
